## Introduction

This https://hotblack944.github.io/retirement-cashflow-modeller is a UK-focused retirement cashflow modeller designed specifically for couples who want to plan their long‑term finances with an emphasis on tax‑efficient drawdown strategies. It brings together all the moving parts of retirement planning and turns them into a single, interactive projection.

![screenshot](screenshot.png)

## What it lets users do
* Enter detailed financial information for two people: ages, salaries, pensions (DC, DB, State), ISAs, and other assets.

* Model salary growth, inflation, investment returns, and spending goals over time.

* Choose a withdrawal strategy, such as taking maximum PCLS early or smoothing income across tax bands.

* Simulate retirement timing, bridge years, and income splitting between partners.

## What it shows
The site generates a full set of long‑term projections, including:

* Annual cashflow: where income comes from each year (salary, drawdown, DB, State Pension, ISA, PCLS) and how it compares to spending needs.

* Asset balances over time: how pensions, ISAs, and other assets grow or deplete.

* Annual tax paid: a year‑by‑year view of combined tax liability.

* Lifetime summaries: total tax paid, total PCLS taken, whether assets ever deplete, and any years with shortfall.
